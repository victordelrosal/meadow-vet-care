## State now

- **Public holidays: shipped.** `check_opening` tool reads the live Irish public holiday calendar
  (Nager.Date, no key). Key correctness point: Nager types Good Friday as `["Bank","School"]`, NOT
  `"Public"`, which is right (it is not one of Ireland's 10 statutory holidays), so we filter on
  `types` and match the official list. Fallback chain: Nager -> OpenHolidays -> baked 2026/27 table.
  Resolves holidays BY NAME too, so the model never invents a date.
- **Weather / dog-walk safety: shipped.** `check_walk_conditions` uses Open-Meteo
  `apparent_temperature` (feels-like). It is a RISK ASSESSMENT, not a temperature chart, because
  the RVC VetCompass research (Hall, Carter & O'Neill 2020, Sci Rep 10:9128) refuses to give a safe
  threshold: median feels-like for UK heatstroke cases was 16.9C, cases from 3.3C to 23.1C, and
  most canine heatstroke is EXERTIONAL. Risk rises for exertion, flat-faced breeds, >=50kg, age
  >=12, overweight, thick coat. Never gives a flat all-clear to a brachy owner.
- **Legal layer: shipped.** `legal.html` + AI-disclosure footer. Covers EU AI Act transparency,
  GDPR (consent-based geolocation, rounded to ~1km, nothing stored), not-veterinary-advice, the
  no-safe-threshold caveat, third-party data, Irish law. Deliberately does NOT contain a consumer
  "waiver": unenforceable under the Unfair Terms Directive / Consumer Rights Act 2022.
- **Simulated market research: run and complete.** 400-client seeded register (Ranelagh, D6),
  40 stratified depth interviews, 12 personas that ACTUALLY used the deployed bot, plus synthesis
  and an adversarial red team. 54 agents, 0 errors. Decoys (pet horoscope, NFT pet ID) rejected by
  all 40, so the endorsement data is trustworthy.
- **Cost estimator: shipped and hardened** (the one feature Victor chose to build). Matches real
  service names, applies live special offers (15%/20%/10% off), states size assumptions, REFUSES to
  guess unknown scenarios, and renders an itemised breakdown card in the UI.
- **Trust defects found by the live sessions and fixed:** the bot used to DEFEND corrupted prices
  ("no typo, €770 is genuinely the live price") because of a system-prompt rule I wrote; it could
  not give the clinic's own phone number; it offered to book then reversed. All fixed.
- Everything is deployed and pushed. Public URL: https://victordelrosal.com/meadow-vet-care/
  Worker: https://meadow-vet-bot.victordelrosal.workers.dev
